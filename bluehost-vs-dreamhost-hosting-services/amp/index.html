Bluehost Vs. DreamHost Hosting Services

Whether it is hosting your web, blog or photo gallery, one thing is of a fact…it has never been easier. There are a number of reasons which can be attributed to this. First of all, we currently have a number of web hosting companies available in the market which is a good thing in terms of competition but leaves customers spoil for choices. We also have free web hosting providers which most customers would love if only they offered quality services. The only disappointing aspect about the free web hosting providers is that they are dangerous in that the probability of them vanishing with all your data is high.

Since it has been established that finding a great web hosting company is always a daunting task, this piece seeks to make a comparison between Bluehost and DreamHost to find out which provider offers the best service in terms of features, pricing, support and availability.

General Take

For quite some time now, Bluehost has remained as one of the most popular and reliable Linux based hosting provide for WordPress. The web hosting company has opted to mainly focus on budget, reliability and high performance as its key strengths. It has been doing this since 1996. Presently, Bluehost hosts about 850,000 WordPress websites even as the numbers continue growing on a monthly basis.

On the other hand DreamHost has also made mark in the industry. The company can also claim to be a veteran in the industry having being founded in 1997. As a web hosting company, DreamHost serves for domain registration, VPS hosting, shared hosting dedicated server and cloud hosting. In as much as the two web hosting companies share some features, they years of service rendering has in deed brought some disparities including:

Price

In terms of price, Bluehost offers the best prices in the market compared with DreamHost. The provider offers a WordPress hosting plan known as ”Plus” which starts at $7.99 per month initially. However, you can now use Bluehost discount coupon to avail discount. The company has recently introduced a new plan dubbed ”Personal plan” that starts at $3.49 per month. This plan is awesome for personal bloggers as it is ideal for them to start a single site while still operating on a budget. DreamHost has plans starting at $8.95 per month making it some how expensive.

Both companies allow for the use of credit cards of Visa, American Express, MasterCard and Discovery. Bluehost goes a notch higher by allowing for easy payment methods such as PayPal. Individuals who have subscribed for Bluehost’s Plus plan are also offered a domain name, up to $200 marketing credit and site builder w/1000 templates.

Features

In as far as features are concerned; the Bluehost Plus plan is quite similar to DreamHost. There are a number of feature that are quite similar including: monthly data transfer, unlimited disk space, MySQL among others. With that said, Bluehost is still one of the richly-featured web hosting companies in the industry, read pros and cons of Bluehost shared hosting.

Bluehost operate with a Bluehost Anytime Money Back Guarantee which provides for a full refund whenever you are in question of their services. With DreamHost, they can only guarantee a 97 days money back. However, they do not give you every penny; they deduct the refund from the account for set up fees, dedication server payments and domain registration fee.

Loading Speed

Web page loading speeds is very critical when it comes to web site. As the web owner, you want to go for a web hosting company that is has the fastest loading speeds. In the last five years, Bluehost developed 3 dedicated data center which in turn have invested over $20 million. The web hosting company has well over 7,500 Dell branded servers that are configured with multiple AMD Operton CPUs, San storage and 32-64GB DDR3 memory. In addition to this, the nationwide fibre connection allows for bandwidth speeds of up to 15,000 MBs leading up to a fast and reliable hosting experience.

DreamHost on the other hand co-locates a data center in Los Angeles. When compared to Bluehost, sites on DreamHost are loading 56% slower.

Bluehost Vs. DreamHost: Conclusion

Whichever way you look at it Bluehost is superior in all means when compared to DreamHost in terms of features, performance and cost.
