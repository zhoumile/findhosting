How to Buy Web Hosting from Bluehost?

We all are aware about the popularity of Bluehost in the web hosting market. It is extensively used by webmasters across the globe; especially by the WordPress users. It is considered one of the most reliable and trustworthy hosting companies. There is tremendous scope for web developers using WordPress platform and this site builder itself strongly recommends its clients to opt for Bluehost Web Hosting. Without much discussion, let me guide you through how to buy web hosting from Bluehost in a precisely comprehensible manner.

Note that, Bluehost has modified its hosting plans. Take a keen look on starter, plus and business pro plans and decide which one you want to go with. The Plus Plan that costs 5.95 USDs/month is the most popular among three due to its affordability in terms of service provision. The Starter Plan that costs 3.49 $/month (apply Bluehost coupon) has limited features and the server space of 100 GB. The Business Pro Plan that costs 13.95 USDs/month is inclusive of strong privacy support and high performance along with the basic features. As per my suggestion, web developers with small-scale projects or commercials can opt for the plus plan whereas the beginners must go with starter plan and the experts holding long-term projects or big commercials can go with the business pro plan.

Visit above link to sign up for Bluehost hosting account. Bluehost provides a free domain option on signing up; you can either register for the same or use your existing one. Do not worry about loss of opportunity; you can take the benefit of free domain name offer later. The screen contains something like shown below. Enter your domain name in first box, if in need, or else enter it in second box to use the existing one. After you are done, click ‘Next’.

The next step is to fill basic account information. This will not take much time. On scrolling down, you will be asked to fill package information i.e. to enter the host plan you have selected. Again downwards, there is billing information section where you will have to enter your credit card or PayPal account details to make the payment. Buying hosting on Bluehost is not a difficult task, but you must ensure the details that you fill are correct.

In order to setup a static website, you need to upload your webpages using the File Manager or FTP account.

For WordPress account holders, the last step is to just login to the CPanel of your account and install WordPress. Thus, you can now begin with taking the advantage of hosting services provided by Bluehost and for WordPress bloggers; it’s going to prove a great success indeed!

At the end of the day, it depends on the web master which plan he finds suitable for his website to get on with. It’s just that you should ensure you don’t simply pay extra bucks for the services you actually don’t find necessary at all. In brief, select a plan that best matches your requirements.
