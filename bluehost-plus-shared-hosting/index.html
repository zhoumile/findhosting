Review of Bluehost Plus Shared Hosting Plan

December 21, 2016 by Samantha Leave a Comment

Review of Bluehost Plus Shared Hosting Plan

Bluehost is a trustworthy name when you need top-notch shared hosting for your upcoming blogs or websites. Despite its reasonable rates, Bluehost hosting services are able to provide the best-quality shared servers. And, to suit different sets of requirements, there are three major packages too. Well, regardless being popular among professionals, you might be a bit dubious to go with the shared hosting packages from Bluehost. To help you with the decision, we shall have a quicker review of Bluehost Plus shared hosting plan. We will discuss the resources you get, available features, pricing etc. At the end, we can hopefully have a clear-cut decision about the plan named Bluehost Plus Shared Hosting.

About Bluehost Shared Hosting

As we mentioned, Bluehost shared hosting is one of the most-used and trusted shared hosting providers in the industry. Not just for affordability, but people love Bluehost services for a variety of reasons. For instance, some people love its top-class customer support while others have an inclination towards worthwhile freebies included. For the sake of selection, Bluehost offers three different shared hosting plans — Basic, Plus and Prime. And, Plus is the most popular choice in that trio. To clear your possible questions about the particular plan, we will discuss the different aspects of Bluehost Plus shared hosting plan.

Pricing

Being the most popular plan, Bluehost Plus shared hosting plan has quite affordable pricing too. Thanks to the available Bluehost discount coupon codes, this plan is available for $4.45 per month at the time of writing. It needs to be noted that there is a discount of 59% applied by default. So, as you can guess, the actual amount may be a bit bigger. But, there’s another thing to be noted. Bluehost Plus Shared Hosting comes with a free domain name for one year. In that section, you can save an amount of something around $10.

That said, if you’re ready to purchase this package from Bluehost, you can get it for just $4.45 per month. In addition, it needs to be noted that rate is applicable only when you purchase the service for three years’ span. If the duration is less, the monthly charge may go high. So, given the availability of the promotion we mentioned, it makes sense to take a leap and purchase the service for three years.

Resources Available

In the trio, Bluehost Plus Shared Hosting plan is the first to offer unlimited domain hosting. Yeah, that’s right. You can host as many websites as you want in this hosting service package. Talking of other resources, you can get unlimited disk space, bandwidth and unlimited subdomains. In short, it is clear that the package is quite suitable for small businesses that have to host multiple websites with easier control. Despite the unlimited availability, Bluehost is offering Standard performance servers for this package instead of the High-Performance once. But, according to what existing users say, performance is more or less fine with Bluehost Plus Shared Hosting package.

Talking of additional features and freebies, Bluehost Plus Shared Hosting offers marketing offers $200 per year. This may include a bunch of credits or offers for advertising, marketing etc. Also, if you are ready to pay $4.45 per year, you will be able to save around $24 each year. It’s so because Bluehost is offering one license of SpamExperts. And, this is an effective way to get rid of spam in your website or blog.

Other Included Features

Some cool features are included in almost all shared hosting packages of Bluehost. A few of the notable ones are:

Regular Backups: Bluehost has an option to take care of daily, weekly and monthly backup of your shared hosting account. This will be useful if something bad happens.

Bluehost is powered by a revamped technology that allocates resources to bring the best results. It isolates those accounts with higher resource consumption, and tries to provide better performance for all websites hosted.

Cloudflare is integrated to each shared hosting package of Bluehost. It means that you can serve your visitors in a better way using this popular Content Delivery Network.

Usability and Support

Bluehost Plus Shared Hosting is powered by the enhanced cPanel – a control panel that has been re-developed by Bluehost for better performance. When compared to the traditional cPanel, Bluehost’s one is pretty minimalistic. It will never be a kind of annoyance to you. On the other hand, cPanel Enhanced is quite effective in terms of desired results. Thanks to this cool User Interface, it’s pretty much simple to use Bluehost Plus Shared Hosting Control Panel.

There is no sort of doubt when it comes to the Support section of Bluehost Plus Shared Hosting. As you can guess, you will have High-Quality support from Bluehost team, via different methods. Let that be Live Chat or Telephone-based support, you can always be confident about clearing your doubts. In other words, it’s best-in-class support that you get.

Bluehost Plus Shared Hosting – Conclusion

Now that we have an idea about different sections of Bluehost Plus Shared Hosting, let’s come down to a decision. In light of what we have seen, Plus plan of Bluehost Shared Hosting is just fine for most users out there. The best part is that it’s no limit in terms of resources or features. For instance, you have unlimited domain hosting and unlimited storage space. Also, pricing is way too affordable, considering the availability of free domain name. Altogether, we find it awesome to go with the Plus plan.
