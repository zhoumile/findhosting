An Introduction to Bluehost WordPress Hosting

As you know, Bluehost is one of the most-trusted and widely-used web hosts in the globe. Currently, Bluehost offers a specialized hosting package for WordPress-powered websites and blogs. It’s sometimes referred to as Managed WordPress Hosting, but its features are same, regardless what you call it. Simply put, Bluehost WordPress Hosting has been heavily optimized to get you the best results in terms of performance and speed. It comes with all the necessary platforms for running WordPress on the server; it will have security-based modifications as to improve WordPress scenario. And, the package will also bring you an awesome control panel that’s quite user-friendly and WordPress-friendly at the same time.

So, this is the basic characteristic and overview of Bluehost WordPress Hosting. Now, we will move on to the several aspects like pricing and performance.

Pricing and Plans

There are four different plans you would find in Bluehost WordPress Hosting section. They have been arranged quite well that anyone can pick according to the need. We shall take a look into the plans.

  • wp standard is the basic plan in the package, priced at $19.99 for the first month. This plan can manage up to 100 Million visits per month and you get a storage space of 30 GB as well. You also get backup storage of 30 GB, in addition to 2 GB RAM. In wp standard package, there’s a free domain included too. It also includes SiteLock CDN, enhanced cPanel and SiteLock Pro for Security.
  • wp enhanced plan of Bluehost WordPress Hosting wants you to pay $29.99 on the first month. It is meant for managing up to 300 Million visits per month and you are provided with 60 GB of storage space and 60 GB for backup needs. Here, this package has a RAM of 4 GB too.
  • wp premium plan is priced at $39.99 for the first month and you will be able to manage up to 600 Million visits each month. There’s a storage space upgrade to 120 GB and you get backup space of same volume with 6 GB RAM. You also get SiteLock Premium and Positive SSL for no extra cost.
  • wp ultimate plan can be bought by paying $49.99 on the first month. This plan can manage unlimited number of visits per month. You have a VPS that offers 240 GB of storage space and 240 GB of backup space. It also is having a RAM of 8 GB. Within this plan, Bluehost has included SiteLock Enterprise and Positive Wildcard SSL. In short, it’s just-right for enterprises.

So, this is the pricing structure of different plans you’d get in Bluehost WordPress Hosting.

Notable Features

It does not matter which plan you go for, you get some cool features from Bluehost. The following features are there in all plans of Bluehost WordPress hosting.

  • Bluehost is using custom-built VPS Servers for these WordPress hosting packages. It’s powered by advanced NGINX architecture and a custom configuration of PHP-FPM. In short, you get best speed from hardware.
  • MOJO Marketplace has been tightly integrated with your package. So, in a few clicks, you will be able to find the right plugins and scripts. Installing these scripts is also an easy task, to be noted.
  • To enhance the security of your WordPress site, there’s SiteLock Security and CDN with almost all packages.

Performance and Speed

You can count on Bluehost servers when it comes to speed and performance. It’s no different as it comes to the case of Bluehost WordPress Hosting either. And, if we look from the software side, there is custom-configured PHP-FPM. So, when you have hosted your WordPress site on Bluehost, you won’t have to worry about uptime, performance or speed.
