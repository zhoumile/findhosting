Bluehost Hosting Vs Free Web Hosting

Most of the times, newbies who want to make their own blogs or websites gets confused between various choices of web hosting. Most of them are running low on budget so, they tend to choose a very cheap hosting or free web hosting service available on the internet. But is it really a wise decision to go with free web hosting for your long term websites? Not at all, actually. We cannot believe a bit on free web hosting providers. It is always better to choose a premium web hosting for your long term needs. Bluehost is one of the oldest and premium web hosting solutions provider. If you are a newbie, and want an easy and hassle free web hosting service, you can opt for Bluehost.

Bluehost is one of the oldest and most trusted web hosting service providers. It is famous for its shared hosting services. You might be thinking why I am recommending Bluehost over free web hosting? There are many solid reasons behind avoiding free web hosting. That’s why, I am writing this comparative explanation on Bluehost hosting Vs. free web hosting.

Bluehost Hosting Vs. Free Web Hosting

  • Features

Bluehost offers many features with their web hosting packages. They offer unlimited disk space, unlimited bandwidth and on some plans, you can host unlimited domains. With these features, you get One-click Auto Installer software preinstalled, using which, you can install various Content Management Scripts (CMS’s) with one click. In addition, you get a free .COM domain with every purchase of web hosting package. With the use of Bluehost coupon code you will save up to 80%, money on your first order with Bluehost hosting.

On free web hosting, you get nothing in features section. Free web hosting providers will give you barely 2 GB disk space, so called 100GB bandwidth and you can host only one domain. But you’ll not able to use these features even if it is mentioned on the registration page. On the other hand, your hosting account will be created on a server on which there are many websites which are hosted along with your website.

  • Reliability

Bluehost has been providing web hosting services since last 20 years. They have earned the trust of customers with great service, even official WordPress is recommending to use Bluehost for the self-hosted WordPress sites. Bluehost service is really reliable that your site will never get down due to server problems. Its strong and fast servers will serve your website to its readers without any problem.

Free hosting services can’t be trusted at any price. You can expect frequent downtimes. Most of the times, free web hosting services inject advertising banners on your websites which may annoy your website visitors. Also, it is common that your site will get deleted if you start receiving good amount of traffic.

  • Security

Bluehost is a premium web hosting provider. They offer high security solutions with every hosting package. Bluehost’s experienced network engineers take care of the safety of your websites. So, you have not to get worried about any security issue.

You get nothing like security on free web hosting services. These hosting services are most unsecure hosting. If you store important data of your website on free web hosting servers, you can never hold it strong. Also, there are many sites which are hosted on a single web server, if anyone from them uploads malicious scripts on server, hackers can easily sneak into server and hack it easily.

  • Customer Support

Bluehost offers fantastic customer support. Their knowledgeable customer support staff is 24/7 online to help you in solving any problem or doubts. You can get in touch with them via Email (Ticket system), live chat, and phone support and also, they’ll solve your troubles if you contact them on Bluehost Facebook page.

Free web hosting doesn’t come with customer support. If some hosting providers, state that they have customer support, it may not be reliable. If you look at popular free web hosting provider 000webhost.com, their customer support replies after taking too much time. You have to be very lucky to get a reply from them.

Verdict

Thus, if you are thinking of utilizing the free web hosting service for your websites, then remember it is not a smart decision. Hosting your websites on free web hosting will be very frustrating and dangerous. Hence, always take a premium web hosting like Bluehost. With Bluehost, you’ll never get frustrated because you’ll get many features, security, reliability and brilliant customer support. I trust, this article will assist you to clear your doubts on free hosting and hopefully, you’ll host your websites on Bluehost.
