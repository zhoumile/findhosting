Bluehost India Vs Bluehost.Com – Complete Review

It’s been a while since Bluehost started offering its services in India through a dedicated Subsidiary. It’s commonly known as Bluehost India and is rich in terms of features offered. Talking of the Bluehost Global, people never doubts its reliability or credibility when it comes to hosting products like Shared Server or VPSs. That having said, as we consider the cases of Bluehost India, there seems to be a small confusion. In short, people are not able to understand the differences between Bluehost India and Bluehost. This issue is faced by web professionals who are from Indian subcontinent.

What do you think about having a point-by-point comparison of Bluehost India vs Bluehost.com? In that way, you will be able to understand the differences and choose well. This is what we are going to do in our article. Shall we start?

About Bluehost India and Bluehost.com

As we said, Bluehost India is the dedicated version of Bluehost for Indian customers. In other words, Bluehost has localized its products and support to suit the needs of customers from India. It’s a matter of fact that Indian customers were facing some issues with payments and all. For instance, not all users had support for international payment methods. As a truly effective answer to all these, Bluehost Global started the Indian subsidiary. If you look at the official website of Bluehost India, you can’t probably spot any differences, except that it’s a .bluehost.com sub domain. However, under the hood, it’s a lot to offer.

You should be pretty familiar with Bluehost.com, which is also known as Bluehost Global or Bluehost US. It’s been in the industry for a huge time, creating an unbeatable brand reputation and build. As you can guess, Bluehost US is accessible from any part of the world. It’s just that you will need to have an international payment method and internet connection. In case if you did not know, Bluehost is owned by Endurance International Group, which is a web hosting conglomerate. It is the same company that owns services like HostGator, iPage and JustHost. And, to make things clear, we must mention that EIG also owns ResellerClub — a popular reseller hosting provider, based in Mumbai, India.

Major Differences in Bluehost India vs Bluehost.com

Now, we will see what the major differences between Bluehost India and Bluehost.com are.

  1. Your Servers are Hosted in India

When you purchase web hosting from Bluehost India, you get servers that are hosted and maintained in India. It works by the tie-up between Bluehost India and ResellerClub, both of which are owned by EIG. So, your website/blog is hosted on the datacenters of ResellerClub, which happens to be in India itself. Obviously, this has both advantages and disadvantages at the same time. First of all, suppose you are receiving most of your traffic from India or other Asian regions. Then, it’s a good thing to have the servers hosted in India. This way, the latency will be low and you can bring better loading speed to visitors.

  1. You Get Local Customer Support

Have you had trouble contacting the Bluehost Global support? If that’s the case, you will definitely love Bluehost India. It is so because you are going to have technical support delivered in regional languages as well. At the least, you can get the support in Hindi, which is a good thing for most people. And the best part is that you can seek telephonic support without actually paying those high ISD rates. Doesn’t that sound cool?

  1. You Can Your Indian Payment Methods

With Bluehost India, you don’t have to wait for international payment methods and all. Almost all kinds of payments will work fine with Bluehost India. This is a major advantage for people who cannot afford all the kinds of international stuff.

What’s the same in Bluehost India and Bluehost.com?

Well, yes, some things don’t change even when you are shifting between the Global version and Indian version.

  1. High-Quality Hardware Support

One reason for Bluehost’s high-performance is the fact that it uses custom-built servers and other hardware. When we do consider the case of Bluehost India, the same high-performance is there too. It means that you don’t have to worry about lack of performance or speed when you are with Bluehost India. In case if you are still doubtful, you can ask users who do host on Bluehost India servers.

  1. Minimal Dashboard

Bluehost Hosting Control Panel is widely acclaimed for its simplicity and power. The design is minimal but has all required control for better management of your server machines. This dashboard has been kept the same in the case of Bluehost India as well. In addition to the cPanel-based control panel, Bluehost India provides another dashboard too. Here, you will have access to the different aspects of your web server. In short, nothing changes here in the case of usability.

By the way, do you want to have US Hosting but also need the perks of being Indian? To suit such needs, Bluehost India is offering US Hosting as well. It means that you can purchase a web server that is hosted in United States. This is a superb option if you receive majority traffic from the US or nearby regions.

Bluehost India vs Bluehost.com — The Final Word on Selection

Apparently, there are several differences between Bluehost India and Bluehost Global. We think the major factor is where your servers are hosted and the support you get. If that’s the case, Indian customers should consider going for Bluehost India. However, it needs to be noted that your site should be receiving most visitors from India or Asia to get the benefit of a Bluehost India server. Otherwise, you can try purchasing US Hosting via Bluehost India. This way, it is possible to get the benefits of local support and payment options — along with the power of US-based web servers.
