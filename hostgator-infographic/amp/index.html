HostGator Infographic

HostGator was founded in 2002 by Brent Oxley, who was then a student at Florida Atlantic University. From then on, the name ‘HostGator’ has been somewhat of a rising star in the web hosting scene. With more than 12,000 servers, hosting around 9,00,000 domains it has become one among the most reliable name. Below is an infographic showing the various features and offers HostGator provides its users which indeed is a main reason behind their rapid growth.

HostGator mainly offers three types of sharing which allows the user to pick exactly what they need and that too at a pretty reasonable cost. More than that they give out HostGator coupons and organize special sales on particular occasions which enable users to get further discounts. They even have a money back guarantee for 45 days without any contract.

However the main reason why many prefer HostGator is because of the wide range of features that it makes available for their users. From the flexible control panel to their award winning support system, everything has been designed in such a way to ensure the friendliest and the most comfortable environment possible for its users.

With unlimited disk space and bandwidth and an uptime guarantee of 99.9% there are few who can match their reliable web hosting features. When it comes to applications, they provide an efficient platform for Joomla and Drupal hosting while also making 75 open source scripts available to be installed just with a click. They even support WordPress hosting to host your very own WordPress blog. Their efficient customer service ensures that you will get your answers whatever your questions may be. With each registration they also give out many free features including free website building tools and Google AdWords offers.
