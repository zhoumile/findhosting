Bluehost Webmail — An Essential Overview for Web Professionals

Obviously, there are a huge number of features that have been helping Bluehost attract various kinds of users to it, for a longer period, ever since its inception, which was more than a decade ago. One of the noteworthy features you can find in Bluehost web hosting services is the presence of web-based email solutions, which is available through an easy-to-use webmail interface. Indeed, people prefer to go with Bluehost because of this feature. In this article, we’d like to give you an overview on Bluehost webmail. Before we move on to the different aspects of the feature, we will tell you why webmail is important in the long run.

 

Why Webmail is Important

When it comes to sending or receiving emails using the email accounts you have, you usually have to make use of an external client such as Microsoft Outlook or Thunderbird. And, in order to make it function, you will have to follow a long-enough configuration process, which would make at least a few of you tired. Also, when you want to check your mails from a friend’s PC or so, this software-based method becomes worst. This is where we find webmail at its most useful form.

Webmail, as the name signifies, brings your email interface in your web browser screen, letting you send and receive messages. Bluehost web hosting is known for the availability of unlimited email accounts that has all the features the situations will require. For instance, when you need to create individual email IDs for all the employees in your firm, it is best to depend upon Bluehost, with which you will get individual login and password. So, after you have created the account and given the details to concerned users, you can sit back and relax, as they can use the webmail-based interface or dedicated email management solution to check their inbox and do other stuff.

So, when you want to let all your employees have an impressive email management solution — using brand name of yours —, you can depend upon Bluehost webmail feature. Now, as you know how useful webmail interface is, we’ll move on to have a detailed overview on the features of Bluehost webmail system.

More About Bluehost Webmail

To power the webmail section of Bluehost hosting control panel, the company offers three different web mail clients that offer ultimate convenience. Available web mail clients in the Bluehost control panel are as follows:

  • RoundCube
  • Horde
  • SquirrelMail

All of these three web mail clients are popular when it comes to managing your email messages! Since Bluehost does have an option to give users individual credentials, you can simply get rid of the tension of setting up an extra place, where your employees can access their inbox. In addition, you will be able to choose the best email web client as per your convenience and requirement. Now, we will have a look on the process of using web mail client in control panel of Bluehost hosting services.

Using Webmail in Bluehost Control Panel

If you are the one who manages cPanel of your Bluehost web hosting service, accessing email inbox using webmail is an easy task, as there will be direct links to webmail section in the dashboard. Once you have selected one, you can move on. On the other hand, when you want to let another person access his or her email account — given that you don’t prefer him accessing the cPanel —, the scenario is a bit different. When you want them to do that, you’ll have to provide a particular URL to the users. Depending upon the web mail client you prefer, you can choose one of the following URLS.

  • For Horde: yourdomainname.com:2095/horde/index.php
  • For RoundCube : yourdomainname.com:2095/3rdparty/roundcube/index.php
  • SquirrelMail : yourdomainname.com:2095/3rdparty/squirrelmail/index.php

We hope you know that you have to change the domain name to your site address! So, you can give these URLs to all the people you want to access email system of your server.
