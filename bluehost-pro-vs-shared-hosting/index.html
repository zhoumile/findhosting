Bluehost Pro Vs. Shared Hosting — Things to Know and Take Care Of

You have heard about Bluehost, as a web developer, blogger, internet marketer or any other professional, who deals with web hosting and related stuff, even though you may not be using it — we are ready for a bet! Well, then, you’d have noticed the absence of multiple shared web hosting plans when it comes to this quite popular web host! On the other hand, there are only three options —the Starter shared hosting or the extended hosting plan that is named Bluehost Pro; amidst they, there is a Plus plan that offers mediocre amount of features and specifications as far as it is a concern of yours to ensure technical perfections.

Nevertheless, it seems that people are still confused between Bluehost Pro and shared hosting, for the former is often referred to as a shared hosting service that gives VPS-based performance for your websites or blogs. In this post, we will have a comparison between Bluehost Pro and standard version of Bluehost shared hosting in such a way that you should know which one you should choose when it comes to a particular instance or so. Here, we will consider several factors into account to reach a conclusion — you can be sure on that.

An Intro — Bluehost Pro Vs. Shared Hosting

Bluehost shared web hosting is, as you all know, one of the best web hosting providers when you seek a perfect web host for your upcoming blog or website that is expected to have a medium level of traffic and bandwidth consumption. The plan costs $3.49 per month with Bluehost coupon codes and offers a lot of resources, regardless the cheapness. The plan lets you host only 1 website, with a storage space of 100GB and unlimited bandwidth. It has to be noted that, when you purchase shared web hosting plan from Bluehost, you have an opportunity to register a domain name for free, which will help you to save a lot of money. Talking of further features, Bluehost shared hosting offers $50-worth resources for marketing as well.

Bluehost Pro is an enhanced upgrade that you can have from the basic version of Bluehost shared hosting! It stands out from a standard upgrade, because Bluehost Pro is notable for the enhanced performance in terms of its server resources and their allocation. Bluehost Pro costs $14.95 per month and offers unlimited resources including number of websites, storage space and bandwidth. And there are $200-worth marketing resources included with the plan so that you would be able to promote your website or blog quite easily than you expect. That is all about plan to be noted, but there’s something more, which we will discuss soon.

These having said, we can say that Bluehost Pro is a type of shared server that a website with high traffic needs. If on the other hand you have just started, standard will do the job, quite neatly.

What Does the ‘Pro’ Offer

There are several factors that make Bluehost Pro servers stand out from those typical shared hosting services though not all are worth praising. The very first thing to be concerned when it comes to choosing the Pro version of Bluehost is the enhanced performance your website or blog gets. Once you have upgraded your plan, your websites will start running web servers that are highly optimized and there will be a controlled amount of resource sharing only. We do hope that’s a big improvement from the user’s point of view, for everyone loves good performance. Thus, that’s it in the performance section.

When it comes to the case of privacy and protection of your web server, Bluehost Pro has some other advanced sort of features. One of those noteworthy features is the presence of a dedicated IP that gives your website a bit stronger identity and authenticity when ranked in SERPs. And, of course, if you think about securing your whole website or at least a part of it using Server Security Layer, you need a dedicated IP to move on. And, yes, Bluehost knows that you might have a plan to implement a SSL and it offers 1 SSL as well. In addition, there’s a Domain Privacy option that will let you keep details about the domain name private. At the same time, you can utilize the 2 SpamExperts accounts to fight with spam in your blog or site.

Another thing that impressed us in Bluehost Pro is the availability of SiteBackup Pro, which will be a great companion when you want to make sure that none of your data is lost due to accidental issues. SiteBackup Pro will keep an eye on the stuff going on. So, we think the Bluehost Pro version is a total upgrade to the performance, security and other aspects of your websites or blog.

The Decision to be Made

We have talked about various things that matter when it comes to comparing Bluehost Pro and standard version, we believe. In light of that, we can see that you should stick on with Bluehost standard version if you’re just starting the website or blog and expect mediocre amount of traffic. On the other hand, after a certain period, you may need an enhanced level of performance and protection and that’s where you have to go for Bluehost Pro!
