Bluehost Vs. HostGator Hosting Services

The same argument can apply to web hosting providers. Today, there are over hundreds of different web hosting providers offering more or less the same services. However, out of the lot, there are only a few that stand out and are highly recommended. The top three web hosting companies that are currently available in the market include: HostGator, Bluehost and DreamHost. There has always been a debate going around relating to which company offers the best web hosting services. In this particular piece, we’ll try to make a comparison between Bluehost and HostGator.

User Interface

Both companies make use of the standard backend known as cPanel. The importance of using user-friendly control panel cannot be overemphasized. It works best for the users who are new to cPanel and are not familiar with the installation of web scripts such as Joomla. To ensure that the user is fully protected, both the companies ensure that their cPanel is given maximum security. This way, individual users can never worry about hackers and any kinds of viruses.

When it comes to the billing areas both hosts are easy to navigate through. What HostGator has attempted to do is; it has tried to customize their cPanel in order to increase customer’s satisfaction. Their interface is slightly easy to use as compared to Bluehost.

Since they both use cPanel, we cannot come up with an outright winner.

Performance and Reliability

In as far as web hosting is concerned, these are the best and without a doubt the most recommended web hosting platforms. The main reason attributed to this is their performance levels. Performance wise, these two offer 99.9% uptime guarantee. HostGator sites have been known to be hosted by Dual Xeon servers in Dallas. They operate with data centers which are well equipped and connected to more than 10 backbone providers. On the other hand, Bluehost uses CPU Segregation technology. The technology has the ability of offering intelligent controls to the CPU and offer protection to the site.

Bluehost has rock solid performance abilities as compared to HostGator. One thing that makes Bluehost to stand out is that the company normally invests millions of dollars year in year out to finance new technologies and offer better performance. Websites that are hosted by Bluehost are known to be capable of supporting thousands of daily users. It is also highly reliable.

Customer Support

Web hosting companies and great customer support goes hand in hand. This is because of the technicalities involved in the industry. Whenever a customer has an issue with their website, the first thing on their minds is to contact the hosting company to seek for solutions. For these reasons, the support team should always be on stand by to offer solutions to the immediate needs of their customers. Immediate solution is perfect when it comes to web sites because it helps prevent site downtime.

Both HostGator and Bluehost offer customer support service around the clock. They have an understand team which is always on stand by to attend to their customers. They can be reached through a number of channels including: emails, phone calls and live chart sessions.

Speed

To determine which web hosting service is faster, a speed test was conducted between HostGator and Bluehost. Before settling for a web hosting company, speed is one of the important factors that should be heavily considered. Pingdom speed testing tool was used to run the test.

Bluehost loads in less than a second. This is great for users as it means that their web pages can load faster.

Bluehost Vs. HostGator: Conclusion

In as much as HostGator is very popular because it has been around for a while, from the comparison above, Bluehost trumps HostGator in many ways. It performs better than HostGator and it is faster when page load time is concerned. With Bluehost, you can always expect rock solid performance.
